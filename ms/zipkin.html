Spring Cloud Sleuth is used for distributed tracing in microservices.

  • It automatically adds Trace IDs and Span IDs to logs.
  • Helps track a request across multiple services.
  • Often used with Zipkin, OpenTelemetry, or Grafana Tempo to visualize trace flows.

Features of Sleuth

  • Auto-configuration of logging with traceId and spanId
  • Works with RestTemplate, WebClient, and messaging tools
  • Adds tracing information in HTTP headers (e.g., X-B3-TraceId)

<dependency>
    <groupId>org.springframework.cloud</groupId>
    <artifactId>spring-cloud-starter-sleuth</artifactId>
</dependency>

                    

spring:
  application:
    name: order-service

  logging:
    pattern:
      level: "%Sp [${spring.application.name:},%X(traceId),%X(spanId)]"
